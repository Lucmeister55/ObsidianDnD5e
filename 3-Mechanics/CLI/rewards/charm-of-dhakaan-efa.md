# Charm of Dhakaan

While you have this Charm, you know the Goblin language. You can expend the Charm to cast [Staggering Smite](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/staggering-smite-xphb.md).

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 51*
