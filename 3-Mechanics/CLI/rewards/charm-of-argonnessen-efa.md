# Charm of Argonnessen

While you have this Charm, you know Draconic. You can expend the Charm to cast [Dragon's Breath](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/dragons-breath-xphb.md) (level 4 version), targeting yourself only.

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 51*
