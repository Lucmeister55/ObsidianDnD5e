# Manifest Charm of Mabar, the Endless Night
*Charm*  

When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that animates or summons an Undead (including [Animate Dead](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/animate-dead-xphb.md), [Create Undead](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/create-undead-xphb.md), or [Summon Undead](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/summon-undead-xphb.md)), you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 50*
