# Charm of Ashtakala

While you have this Charm, you know Abyssal and Infernal. You can expend the Charm to cast [Ray of Sickness](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/ray-of-sickness-xphb.md) (level 4 version).

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 51*
