# Charm of Khyber

While you have this Charm, you have [Darkvision](/3-Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Darkvision) with a range of 120 feet. You can expend the Charm to gain [Truesight](/3-Mechanics/CLI/senses.md#Truesight) with a range of 60 feet for 1 minute.

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 51*
