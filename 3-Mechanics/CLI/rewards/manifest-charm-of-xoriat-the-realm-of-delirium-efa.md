# Manifest Charm of Xoriat, the Realm of Delirium
*Charm*  

When you use a spell slot to cast a spell, you can expend the Charm (no action required) to roll on the [Wild Magic Surge table](/3-Mechanics/CLI/tables/wild-magic-surge-xphb.md) in the "Player's Handbook".

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 50*
