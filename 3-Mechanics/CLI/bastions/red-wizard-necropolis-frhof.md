# Red Wizard Necropolis
*Level 5 Bastion facility*  

- **Prerequisites**: Ability to use a [Spellcasting Focus](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/spellcasting-focus-xphb.md), Membership in the Red Wizards
- **Space**: Roomy (16 sq)
- **Hirelings**: 1
- **Order**: recruit

Red Wizards defend their strongholds with Undead minions. A Red Wizard Necropolis can accommodate up to eight Bastion Defenders.

## Recruit: Undead Defenders

Each time you issue the Recruit action to this facility, up to four Undead Bastion Defenders are created and take shelter in the crypts and burial chambers of your Red Wizard Necropolis. You can't issue the Recruit order to this facility if it's fully occupied.

Keep track of Undead Bastion Defenders you lose and deduct them from your Bastion Defenders roster. An Undead Bastion Defender returns to your Red Wizard Necropolis 14 days after being destroyed. Undead Bastion Defenders can't return from being killed if the facility is shut down or fully occupied.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 177*
