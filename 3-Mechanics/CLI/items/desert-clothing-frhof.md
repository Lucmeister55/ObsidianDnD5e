# Desert Clothing
*Adventuring gear*  
![](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/img/desert-clothing.webp#right)

- **Cost**: 10 gp
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

*Stylish but practical, our desert clothing protects you whether you're taking a caravan of camels through the Calim Desert or exploring an ancient Mulhorandi tomb.*

When you are wearing Desert Clothing and not wearing Medium or Heavy armor, you automatically succeed on saving throws against the effects of [extreme heat](/3-Mechanics/CLI/traps-hazards/extreme-heat-xdmg.md).

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
