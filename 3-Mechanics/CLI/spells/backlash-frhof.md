# Backlash
*4th-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Reaction, which you take in response to taking damage
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You ward yourself against destructive energy, reducing the damage taken by `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`) plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

If the triggering damage was from a creature within range, you can force the creature to make a Constitution saving throw. The creature takes `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`) Force damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage reduction and Force damage from this spell both increase by `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(1d6)` for every spell slot level above 4.
