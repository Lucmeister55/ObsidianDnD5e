# Thunderclap
*cantrip, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (5-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Each creature in a 5-foot [Emanation](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) Thunder damage. The spell's thunderous sound can be heard up to 100 feet away.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) when you reach levels 5 (`dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`)), 11 (`dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d6`)), and 17 (`dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`)).
