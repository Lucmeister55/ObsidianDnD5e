# Death Armor
*2nd-level, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (an onyx worth 50+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

For the duration, an inky aura surrounds one creature you touch. The target has [Advantage](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on [Death Saving Throws](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/death-saving-throw-xphb.md), and once per turn, when a creature within 5 feet of the target hits it with a melee attack roll, the attacker takes `dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d4`) Necrotic damage.
