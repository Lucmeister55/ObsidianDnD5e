# Poison Spray
*cantrip, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You spray toxic mist at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes `dice:1d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d12`) Poison damage.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `dice:1d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d12`) when you reach levels 5 (`dice:2d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d12`)), 11 (`dice:3d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d12`)), and 17 (`dice:4d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d12`)).
