# Alustriel's Mooncloak
*5th-level, Abjuration*  
![Alustriel Silverhand uses ...](file://D:\ProgramData\CLI\5etools-img/spells/FRHoF/Alustriel's Mooncloak.webp#right "Alustriel Silverhand uses her mooncloak to protect her allies.")

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (20-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a moonstone worth 50+ GP)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

For the duration, moonlight fills a 20-foot [Emanation](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you with [Dim Light](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md). While in that area, you and your allies have [Half Cover](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md) and [Resistance](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Cold, Lightning, and Radiant damage.

While the spell lasts, you can use one of the following options, ending the spell immediately:

- **Liberation.** When you fail a saving throw to avoid or end the [Frightened](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Frightened), [Grappled](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Grappled), or [Restrained](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Restrained) condition, you can take a [Reaction](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to succeed on the save instead.  
- **Respite.** As a [Magic](/3-Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Magic) action, you or an ally within the area regains [Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to `dice:4d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d10`) plus your spellcasting ability modifier.  

**Classes**: [Bard](/3-Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Druid](/3-Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Ranger](/3-Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-ranger.md); [Wizard (Abjurer)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-abjurer-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Wizard](/3-Mechanics/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-wizard.md)
