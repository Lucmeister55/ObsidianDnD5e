# Emerald Enclave Renown
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 156* 

**Emerald Enclave Renown**

| Renown Score | Reward |
|--------------|--------|
| 3+ | You can hire Emerald Enclave hirelings for half the usual cost. |
| 5+ | You can request a Caretaker ([Scout](/3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/scout-xmm.md)) to accompany you on a journey and help you navigate, forage, and search. Once you use this reward, you can't do so again until your Renown Score increases. |
| 10 | The first time your Renown Score reaches 10, a Caretaker teaches you Druidic. |
| 15+ | You can buy Common and Uncommon potions and [Spell Scrolls](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/spell-scroll-xdmg.md) from the Emerald Enclave. |
| 25+ | If you or your companions are at risk of suffering from dehydration or malnutrition while within 10 miles of an enclave site, Caretakers bring you enough food and water to meet your needs. |
| 30 | The first time your Renown Score reaches 30, Caretakers provide you with `dice:1d4\|noform\|noparens\|avg` (`1d4`) [Potions of Healing](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/potion-of-healing-xdmg.md) (superior). |
| 40+ | Your eyes turn bright green. Centaurs, treants, unicorns, and all Beasts are Friendly to you by default. |
| 50+ | Members of the Emerald Enclave openly share information with you. In addition, you can request a single audience with the Three. Once you use this reward, you can't do so again. |
