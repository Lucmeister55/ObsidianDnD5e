# Greater Mark of Finding
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 43*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Hunter's Intuition benefit of your [Mark of Finding](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-finding-efa.md) feat, you can roll `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) instead of `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`).

**Improved Finding.** When you use the Finder's Magic benefit of your [Mark of Finding](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-finding-efa.md) to cast [Hunter's Mark](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/hunters-mark-xphb.md) without a spell slot, the range of the spell is doubled, and you can modify the spell so that the target can't benefit from the [Invisible](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Invisible) condition for the duration.
