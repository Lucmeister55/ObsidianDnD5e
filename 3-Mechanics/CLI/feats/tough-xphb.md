# Tough
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 202*  

Your [Hit Point](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your character level when you gain this feat. Whenever you gain a character level thereafter, your [Hit Point](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) maximum increases by an additional 2 [Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md).
