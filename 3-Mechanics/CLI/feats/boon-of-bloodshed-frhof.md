# Boon of Bloodshed
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 42*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Killer's Fortune.** When an enemy you can see is reduced to 0 [Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), you gain [Advantage](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on the next attack roll you make before the end of your next turn.

**Power from Pain.** Once per turn, when you make an attack roll while [Bloodied](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Bloodied), you can deal extra damage to the target equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md). The extra damage's type is the same as the attack's type.
