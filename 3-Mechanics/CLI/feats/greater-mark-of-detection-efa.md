# Greater Mark of Detection
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 43*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Deductive Intuition benefit of your [Mark of Detection](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-detection-efa.md) feat, you can roll `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) instead of `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`).

**Shared Detection.** When you use the Magical Detection benefit of your [Mark of Detection](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-detection-efa.md) feat to cast [See Invisibility](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/see-invisibility-xphb.md) without a spell slot, you can choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself. That creature also gains the benefits of the spell for its duration.
