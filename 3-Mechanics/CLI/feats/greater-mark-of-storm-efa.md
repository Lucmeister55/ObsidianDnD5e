# Greater Mark of Storm
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 45*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Windwright's Intuition benefit of your [Mark of Storm](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-storm-efa.md) feat, you can roll `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) instead of `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`).

**Improved Storm.** When you use the Storm Magic benefit of your [Mark of Storm](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-storm-efa.md) feat to cast [Gust of Wind](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/gust-of-wind-xphb.md) without a spell slot, you also gain a [Fly Speed](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/fly-speed-xphb.md) of 60 feet for the duration of the spell.
