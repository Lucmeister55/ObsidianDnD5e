# Genie Magic
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 39*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Wish Magic.** As a [Magic](/3-Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Magic) action, you can cast a level 1 spell of your choice from the Sorcerer spell list that has a casting time of an action. Once you use this benefit, you can't do so again until you finish a [Long Rest](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). The spell's spellcasting ability is the ability increased by this feat.

When you reach level 11, the spell you cast with this feat is cast as though using a level 2 spell slot.

When you reach level 17, the spell is cast as though using a level 3 spell slot.
