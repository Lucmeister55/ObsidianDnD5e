# Mark of Sentinel
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 41*  

**Prerequisite**: Eberron Campaign, Can't Have Another D Feat

You gain the following benefits.

**Sentinel's Intuition.** When you make a Wisdom ([Insight](/3-Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Insight) or [Perception](/3-Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Perception)) check, you can roll `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`) and add the number rolled to the ability check.

**Guardian's Shield.** You always have the [Shield](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/shield-xphb.md) spell prepared. You can cast it once without a spell slot, and you regain the ability to cast it in that way when you finish a [Long Rest](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). You can also cast it using any spell slots you have. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell (choose when you select this feat).

**Vigilant Guardian.** When a creature you can see within 5 feet of you is hit by an attack roll, you can take a [Reaction](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to swap places with that creature, and you are hit by the attack instead. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md) and regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

**Spells of the Mark.** If you have the Spellcasting or Pact Magic class feature, the spells on the Mark of Sentinel Spells table are added to that feature's spell list.
