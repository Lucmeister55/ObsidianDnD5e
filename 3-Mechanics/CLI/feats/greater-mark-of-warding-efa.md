# Greater Mark of Warding
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 45*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Warder's Intuition benefit of your [Mark of Warding](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-warding-efa.md) feat, you can roll `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) instead of `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`).

**Improved Warding.** When a creature makes an attack roll against you or a creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself, you can take a [Reaction](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to impose [Disadvantage](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on that roll. You can use this benefit a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
