# Cold Caster
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 39*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Cantrip.** You learn the [Ray of Frost](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/ray-of-frost-xphb.md) cantrip. If you already know it, you learn a different Wizard cantrip of your choice. The spell's spellcasting ability is the ability increased by this feat.

**Frostbite.** Once per turn when you hit a creature with an attack roll and deal Cold damage, you can temporarily negate the creature's defenses. The creature subtracts `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`) from the next saving throw it makes before the end of your next turn.
