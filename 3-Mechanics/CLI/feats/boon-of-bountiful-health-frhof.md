# Boon of Bountiful Health
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 42*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Augmented Health.** When you gain [Temporary Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md), increase the number of [Temporary Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) you gain by 5.

**Superior Recuperation.** When you spend one or more [Hit Point Dice](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md) to regain [Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), you can instead use the highest number possible for each die.
