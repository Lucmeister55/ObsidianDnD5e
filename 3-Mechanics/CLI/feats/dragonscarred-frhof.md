# Dragonscarred
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 39*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Constitution or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Damage Resistance.** When you gain this feat, choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Poison. You have [Resistance](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to the chosen damage type.

**Fearsome Power.** When you deal damage to a creature as part of the Attack or Magic action on your turn, you can use the Dragon's Terror benefit of the Cult of the Dragon Initiate feat as a [Bonus Action](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) this turn.
