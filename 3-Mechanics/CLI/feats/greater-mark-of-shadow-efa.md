# Greater Mark of Shadow
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 45*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Cunning Intuition benefit of your [Mark of Shadow](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-shadow-efa.md) feat, you can roll `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) instead of `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`).

**Improved Shadow.** When you use the Shape Shadows benefit of your [Mark of Shadow](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-shadow-efa.md) feat to cast [Invisibility](/3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/invisibility-xphb.md) without a spell slot, you cast the spell as its level 3 version.
