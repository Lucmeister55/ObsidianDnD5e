# House Orien Heir
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 30*  
![](/3-Mechanics/CLI/backgrounds/img/house-orien-heir.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores.** Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence  
- **Feat.** [Mark of Passage](/3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mark-of-passage-efa.md)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Acrobatics](/3-Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Acrobatics) and [Athletics](/3-Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Athletics)  
- **Tool Proficiencies.** [Cartographer's Tools](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/cartographers-tools-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Cartographer's Tools](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/cartographers-tools-xphb.md), [Fine Clothes](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/fine-clothes-xphb.md), [Map](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/map-xphb.md), [Map or Scroll Case](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/map-or-scroll-case-xphb.md), 18 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

Before the Last War, Orien's influence covered Khorvaire, and its trade roads and lightning rails were the lifeblood of a vibrant kingdom. But the war cut those arteries, leaving Galifar dead and House Orien bloodied. While the house's couriers and shippers still keep goods moving on both sides of the continent, finding a way to reestablish routes across the Mournland remains the house's top priority. In the meantime, Orien adventurers can serve their house by investigating missing couriers, recovering goods stolen from caravans, and troubleshooting disruptions to the lightning rail.
