# Game format

- Rules: PHB 2024
- Main classes: PHB 2024
- Subclasses: PHB 2024 + expanded
- Spells: PHB 2024 + expanded
# Characters

The game will start with each player choosing a precreated origin character based on their appearance, class and some general themes. After the first act, players will get to customize various aspects of the character, including things such as gender, etc (at the DM’s discretion). The names and genders referenced in the document dedicated to them are merely codenames. If any players don’t like their characters at all, they can either swap characters with other players, or create entirely new ones (not recommended, but possible).
