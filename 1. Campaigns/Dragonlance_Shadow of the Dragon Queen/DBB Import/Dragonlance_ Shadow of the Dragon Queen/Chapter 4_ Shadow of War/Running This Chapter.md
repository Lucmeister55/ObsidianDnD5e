# Running This Chapter
This chapter begins with an overview of the city of Kalaman. After familiarizing yourself with the “[[Kalaman Gazetteer]]” section, continue the adventure with the characters and Vogler’s refugees arriving near the city. Once the characters ensure the villagers’ safety, encourage them to follow Darrett Highwater’s lead by joining—or at least collaborating with—Kalaman’s military. The military provides the support and direction necessary to face the Red Dragon Army and its supernatural allies for the rest of the adventure.

### Character Advancement

In this chapter, level advancement is handled as follows:

*   Characters start this chapter at 4th level, having gained a level after evacuating Vogler.
*   Characters advance to 5th level [[Missions for Kalaman#Character Advancement|before retaking Wheelwatch Outpost]].
*   Characters advance to 6th level after defeating the skeletal knight Zanas Sarlamir at the [[Raided Catacombs#Next Steps|end of the chapter]].
